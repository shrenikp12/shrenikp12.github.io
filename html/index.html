GUN DEATHS IN AMERICA (2006-2020)

Visualization of American gun deaths over the years from 2006 to 2020

This visualization shows the amount of American gun deaths over the years. Each death is represented by a small red dot on the screen. As the year updates, all the people who died from guns that year are added as new red dots to the screen. The data covers the years 2006 through 2020. Press Play to start the visualization and Pause to stop it at any time. Note that due to the massive amounts of data being displayed, the visualization may take a few minutes to complete. Once started, scroll down slightly to see the full box
